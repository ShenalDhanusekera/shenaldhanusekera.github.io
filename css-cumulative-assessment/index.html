SISLER GAME ON 2019

Sisler Game On!

Game On is a collaborative game jam which teaches students from grade 4 – 12, coding, prototyping, game design and art from local and national industry leaders. This two-day celebration of techno-creative skills is open to all Manitoba schools and put on by Sisler High School's Interactive Digital Media Program.

TECHNOVATION CAMP 2019

Technovation Camp!

Pembina Trails School Division recognizes the importance of technology and innovative thinking in a 21st century learner. The division is once again excited to announce a unique opportunity for students who show great interest and promise in this field.The PTEC TECHNOVATION CAMP will be held on February 7 & 8.

SMARTPARK 2019

Moving To Smartpark!

PTEC is officially a school in Pembina Trails School Division and we are excited to welcome our 2019-2020 students to their brand new classroom located on the University of Manitoba's campus, SMARTPARK. We are very optimistic for the unique learning opportunity that our students have in their future.
